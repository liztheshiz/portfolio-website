About Me

Hi! My name is Liz Stone, and I'm a web developer currently based in Boston, MA.

I started programming in high school, drawn by a love of video games and logical geometry proofs (no joke). But there weren't many tech opportunities available in my tiny town in Montana, so I packed up and headed for Cornell University in Ithaca, NY, where I continued to study computer science out-of-major and worked on various video game projects for fun.

A few years after graduating and working odd jobs in NY and MA, I decided to take my interest in coding more seriously, and enrolled in Career Foundry's Web Development course - and here we are! The tools I've learned there I first applied to this very website!

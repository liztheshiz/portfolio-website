About Me

Hi! My name is Liz Stone, and I'm a web developer and soundtrack artist currently based in Boston, MA.

I've been programming and composing since high school, originally drawn to both activities by a love of gaming, math, and music. After graduation, there weren't many applicable opportunities available in my tiny hometown in Montana, so I packed up and headed for Cornell University in Ithaca, NY, where I continued to study computer science and music out-of-major, and coded and composed for various video game and soundtrack projects for fun. (Check out some of those projects here!)

A few years after graduating and working odd jobs in NY and MA, I decided to take my interest in coding more seriously, and enrolled in Career Foundry's Web Development course - and here we are! The tools I've learned there I first applied to this very website!

I continue to compose and write music in my spare time, and am always interested in taking on new projects. You can get in touch with me here if you have a project you'd like me to work on with you!
